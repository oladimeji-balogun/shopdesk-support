# ShopDesk Customer Support — FAQ Knowledge Base

## Shipping & Delivery

**How long does standard shipping take?**
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days from the date your order is confirmed. Business days exclude weekends and public holidays. You will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number once your order has been dispatched from our warehouse.

**Do you offer express shipping?**
Yes. Express shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee and delivers within 1–2 business days. Express orders placed before 12:00 PM (noon) on a business day are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after noon or on weekends are dispatched the next business day. Express shipping is only available for domestic orders within the United States.

**Do you ship internationally?**
ShopDesk currently ships to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. International orders typically arrive within 10–14 business days depending on the destination country and local customs processing times. ShopDesk is not responsible for customs duties or import taxes that may be imposed by your country's customs authority — these are the responsibility of the recipient.

**What happens if my package is delayed?**
Occasional delays can occur due to high demand periods, severe weather disruptions, carrier backlogs, or customs processing for international orders. If your order has not arrived within 3 business days of the estimated delivery date, please contact our support team with your order ID and we will open an investigation with the carrier on your behalf.

**Is shipping free?**
Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States. Orders below this threshold incur a flat shipping fee of $5.99 for standard delivery and $14.99 for express delivery. Free shipping promotions may be offered during sale periods and will be clearly communicated at checkout.

**Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?**
Shipping address changes can only be accommodated within 2 hours of placing your order, provided the order has not yet been processed for dispatch. Contact our support team immediately with your order ID and the correct address. Once an order has been dispatched, we are unable to redirect it and you will need to arrange redelivery directly with the carrier.

**What carriers does ShopDesk use?**
ShopDesk partners with UPS, FedEx, and USPS for domestic deliveries and DHL for international shipments. The carrier assigned to your order depends on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout. Carrier information is included in your dispatch confirmation email.

**What happens if nobody is home when my package is delivered?**
For orders that require a signature, the carrier will leave a delivery attempt notice and try again the following business day. After two failed attempts, the package will be held at the nearest carrier facility for up to 7 days before being returned to us. If a package is returned due to failed delivery attempts, we will contact you to arrange reshipment. A reshipment fee may apply.

## Order Tracking

**How do I track my order?**
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an email containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. You can also log into your ShopDesk account, navigate to "My Orders," and click on any order to view its current status and real-time tracking information.

**My tracking number is not working. What should I do?**
Tracking numbers can take up to 24 hours to become active in the carrier's system after you receive the dispatch email. If your tracking number is still not working after 24 hours, please contact our support team and we will verify the shipment details and provide an update.

**What does each order status mean?**
- **Pending:** Your order has been received and is awaiting processing by our warehouse team.
- **Processing:** Our warehouse team is preparing your order for dispatch.
- **Shipped:** Your order has left our warehouse and is in transit with the carrier.
- **Out for Delivery:** Your order is with the local delivery driver and will arrive today.
- **Delivered:** Your order has been marked as delivered by the carrier.
- **Cancelled:** Your order has been cancelled and any payment has been refunded.
- **Returned:** Your order has been returned to our warehouse and a refund is being processed.

**My tracking shows delivered but I haven't received my package. What should I do?**
First, check around your property including any porches, side doors, or with neighbors, as carriers sometimes leave packages in safe spots. Also check with other members of your household. If the package is still missing after 24 hours of the delivered status, contact our support team. We will file a missing package claim with the carrier. Investigations typically take 3–5 business days. If the carrier confirms the package is lost, we will offer a full replacement or refund.

**Can I track my order without an account?**
Yes. You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your dispatch confirmation email by visiting the carrier's website directly. However, for full order details and history, a ShopDesk account is required.

## Cancellations

**Can I cancel my order?**
You can cancel your order within 12 hours of placing it, provided it has not yet been dispatched. To cancel, contact our support team as soon as possible with your order ID. Once an order has been shipped, it can no longer be cancelled and must go through our return process instead.

**How do I know if my cancellation was successful?**
You will receive a cancellation confirmation email once your request has been processed. If you do not receive this email within 1 hour of your cancellation request, contact our support team to confirm the status.

**How long does a cancellation refund take?**
Once a cancellation is confirmed, refunds are processed within 2–3 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider and typically takes an additional 3–5 business days after processing.

**Can I cancel part of my order?**
If your order contains multiple items, you may be able to cancel individual items rather than the entire order, provided those items have not yet been dispatched. Contact our support team with your order ID and specify which items you wish to cancel.

**What if I placed a duplicate order by mistake?**
Contact our support team immediately with both order IDs. If neither order has been dispatched, we can cancel one of them and process a full refund. If one has already been dispatched, you will need to return it through our standard return process once it arrives.

## Returns & Refunds

**What is your return policy?**
ShopDesk accepts returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Certain items are not eligible for return including digital products, gift cards, perishable goods, intimate apparel, and items marked as final sale at the time of purchase.

**How do I initiate a return?**
To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order ID and the reason for the return. We will provide you with a prepaid return shipping label via email within 1 business day. Package the item securely, attach the label, and drop it off at any authorized carrier location. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days.

**Will I be refunded the full amount?**
If the return is due to a defective product, a missing item, or an error on our part, you will receive a full refund including any original shipping charges paid. If the return is for personal reasons such as a change of mind or ordering the wrong size, the original shipping fee is non-refundable and a restocking fee of $2.99 may apply.

**What if I received a damaged or defective item?**
If you received a damaged or defective item, contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. Include your order ID and clear photos showing the damage or defect. We will review your case and offer either a full replacement shipped at no cost or a full refund including original shipping charges. You will not be required to return the damaged item in most cases.

**What if I received the wrong item?**
If you received an item different from what you ordered, contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery with your order ID and a photo of the item received. We will arrange for the correct item to be shipped to you immediately and provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item at no cost to you.

**Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?**
ShopDesk does not currently offer direct exchanges. To get a different item, initiate a return for the original item and place a new order for the replacement. If you are concerned about stock availability, contact our support team and we can advise on current inventory.

**How will my refund be issued?**
Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used at checkout. We are unable to redirect refunds to a different card or account. If your original payment method is no longer active, contact our support team and we will work with you to find an alternative solution.

## Payments

**What payment methods do you accept?**
ShopDesk accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely through our payment partners and your card details are never stored on our servers.

**Is it safe to pay on ShopDesk?**
Yes. All payments on ShopDesk are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways and protected by 256-bit SSL encryption. We do not store your full card details. All transactions are monitored for suspicious activity to protect your account.

**Why was my payment declined?**
Payments can be declined for several reasons including insufficient funds, incorrect card details, an expired card, or your bank flagging the transaction as unusual activity. We recommend double-checking your payment details or contacting your bank to authorize the transaction. If the problem persists, try a different payment method or contact our support team for assistance.

**Can I pay in installments?**
ShopDesk does not currently offer installment payment plans. All orders must be paid in full at the time of checkout. We are exploring buy-now-pay-later options and will announce any additions to our payment methods via email and our website.

**I was charged twice for the same order. What should I do?**
Double charges can occasionally occur due to payment processing errors. Contact our support team immediately with your order ID and the dates of both charges. We will investigate and issue a refund for the duplicate charge within 2–3 business days. You may also see a pending authorization hold from your bank which typically disappears within 3–5 business days if the charge did not go through.

**Can I get a VAT or tax invoice?**
Yes. Tax invoices are available for all completed orders. Log into your account, navigate to "My Orders," select the relevant order, and click "Download Invoice." If you require a customized invoice for business purposes, contact our support team with your order ID and business details.

## Promotions, Discounts & Coupon Codes

**How do I apply a coupon code?**
At checkout, enter your coupon code in the "Promo Code" field and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total before payment. Only one coupon code can be applied per order. Coupon codes cannot be applied retroactively to completed orders.

**My coupon code is not working. What should I do?**
Coupon codes may fail for several reasons: the code may have expired, already been used, not meet the minimum order value requirement, or apply only to specific product categories. Check the terms and conditions of your code. If you believe the code should be valid, contact our support team with the code and your order details and we will investigate.

**Can I use multiple coupon codes on one order?**
No. ShopDesk allows only one coupon code per order. If you have multiple codes, choose the one that gives you the greatest discount. Coupon codes also cannot be combined with other ongoing promotional pricing unless explicitly stated.

**Do you offer a first-time buyer discount?**
Yes. New ShopDesk customers receive a 10% discount on their first order. This discount is automatically applied at checkout when you create a new account and make your first purchase. The first-time buyer discount cannot be combined with other coupon codes.

**How do I find out about current promotions and sales?**
Subscribe to our newsletter to receive exclusive promotions, early sale access, and coupon codes directly to your inbox. You can also follow ShopDesk on our social media channels or check the "Deals" section of our website for current offers.

**Can I get a price adjustment if an item goes on sale after I purchase it?**
ShopDesk offers price adjustments within 7 days of your purchase if the same item goes on sale on our website. Contact our support team with your order ID and the current sale price and we will issue a store credit for the difference. Price adjustments do not apply to flash sales, limited-time deals, or Black Friday and Cyber Monday promotions.

**Do coupon codes expire?**
Yes. All coupon codes have an expiry date which is communicated when the code is issued. Expired codes cannot be reactivated. Gift cards, however, do not expire and retain their full value indefinitely.

**Can I get a refund on a discounted item?**
Yes. If you return an item purchased with a coupon code, you will be refunded the amount you actually paid after the discount was applied — not the original price. The coupon code itself is not reinstated after a return.

## Disputes, Fraud & Missing Packages

**I think my account has been hacked. What should I do?**
If you suspect unauthorized access to your ShopDesk account, change your password immediately via the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. Then contact our support team right away. We will review recent account activity, flag any suspicious orders, and temporarily secure your account while we investigate. We recommend also changing your password on any other services where you use the same credentials.

**I see an order on my account that I did not place. What should I do?**
Contact our support team immediately with the order ID of the unauthorized order. Do not attempt to cancel it yourself. We will investigate the activity, cancel and refund the unauthorized order if it has not yet been shipped, and take steps to secure your account. If a package has already been shipped, we will work with you and the carrier to intercept it where possible.

**I never received my package but tracking says it was delivered. What can I do?**
Start by checking around your property, with neighbors, and with other household members. Carriers sometimes leave packages in unexpected but safe locations. If the package is still missing 24 hours after the delivery status was updated, contact our support team. We will file an official missing package investigation with the carrier. This process typically takes 3–5 business days. If the carrier is unable to locate the package, we will issue a full replacement or refund.

**My package arrived open or appeared tampered with. What should I do?**
Do not discard the packaging. Take photos of the package and its contents as received. Contact our support team within 48 hours with your order ID and photos. We will determine whether the tampering occurred during transit or before dispatch and will arrange a replacement or refund accordingly. If the tampering appears criminal in nature, we may advise you to file a report with your local postal authority.

**I received someone else's package. What should I do?**
Do not open the package if it is addressed to someone else. Contact our support team with your order ID and details of the package you received. We will arrange for the correct package to be sent to you and provide instructions for returning the mislabeled one at no cost to you.

**What should I do if I suspect a fraudulent seller on ShopDesk?**
If you believe a seller listing on ShopDesk is fraudulent or misrepresenting their products, report it to our support team with the product link and a description of your concern. Our trust and safety team will investigate and take appropriate action. ShopDesk does not tolerate fraudulent listings and maintains a zero-tolerance policy for seller misrepresentation.

**Can I dispute a charge with my bank instead of going through ShopDesk?**
You are entitled to file a chargeback with your bank or card provider; however, we strongly encourage you to contact our support team first. We resolve the vast majority of payment disputes faster than the bank chargeback process, often within 2–3 business days. Filing a chargeback while a ShopDesk investigation is already open may complicate and delay your resolution.

## Seller & Vendor Policies

**How do I become a seller on ShopDesk?**
To apply as a vendor on ShopDesk, visit our Seller Portal at shopdesk.com/sell and complete the application form. You will need to provide your business name, registered address, tax identification number, and bank account details for payouts. Applications are reviewed within 5–7 business days and you will be notified of the outcome via email.

**What are the requirements to sell on ShopDesk?**
All sellers must be registered businesses with a valid tax ID. Individual sellers are not currently accepted. Products must comply with ShopDesk's prohibited items policy, which includes restrictions on counterfeit goods, hazardous materials, age-restricted products, and items that violate intellectual property rights. Full seller requirements are available in the Seller Handbook on our website.

**How and when do sellers get paid?**
Sellers receive payouts on a bi-weekly basis for all orders that have been delivered and where the return window has closed. Payouts are transferred directly to the bank account registered in your seller profile. A detailed breakdown of each payout including fees and deductions is available in your Seller Dashboard.

**What fees does ShopDesk charge sellers?**
ShopDesk charges a platform commission of 8–15% per sale depending on the product category. Additionally, a payment processing fee of 2.9% + $0.30 applies to each transaction. There are no monthly listing fees. Sellers who exceed $50,000 in annual sales may qualify for reduced commission rates through our Preferred Seller Program.

**What happens if a customer returns an item purchased from my store?**
When a customer returns an item, the refund is deducted from your next payout cycle along with the original commission. If the return is due to a seller error such as shipping the wrong item or a defective product, an additional penalty fee may apply per ShopDesk's seller performance policy. Sellers with a return rate above 10% may be subject to account review.

**Can ShopDesk remove my listing?**
Yes. ShopDesk reserves the right to remove any listing that violates our policies, receives a significant number of customer complaints, or is flagged by our trust and safety team. Sellers will be notified of any listing removal with a reason provided. Repeated violations may result in seller account suspension or permanent termination.

**How do I handle a customer dispute as a seller?**
When a customer raises a dispute related to your store, you will receive a notification in your Seller Dashboard. You have 48 hours to respond with evidence such as dispatch confirmation, tracking details, and photos of the item before dispatch. If you do not respond within 48 hours, ShopDesk may resolve the dispute in favor of the customer automatically.

**What is ShopDesk's policy on counterfeit goods?**
ShopDesk has a strict zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit or replica products. Any seller found listing counterfeit goods will have their account permanently terminated and may be reported to the relevant authorities. Buyers who receive suspected counterfeit items should contact our support team immediately.

## Subscription & Membership Plans

**Does ShopDesk offer a membership plan?**
Yes. ShopDesk Plus is our premium membership plan available for $9.99 per month or $89.99 per year (a saving of approximately 25%). Members enjoy free standard shipping on all orders regardless of order value, early access to sales, exclusive member-only coupon codes, and priority customer support.

**How do I sign up for ShopDesk Plus?**
You can subscribe to ShopDesk Plus from your account settings under "Membership." Select either the monthly or annual plan, enter your payment details, and your membership activates immediately. Your first billing cycle begins on the day you subscribe.

**Can I cancel my ShopDesk Plus membership?**
Yes. You can cancel your ShopDesk Plus membership at any time from your account settings. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period — you will continue to enjoy membership benefits until then. ShopDesk Plus subscriptions are not refunded on a pro-rata basis for unused days in the current billing period.

**What happens to my membership benefits if I cancel?**
Once your membership period ends following cancellation, you will lose access to all ShopDesk Plus benefits including free shipping, exclusive discounts, and priority support. Any orders placed before cancellation that are still in transit will not be affected.

**Is ShopDesk Plus available internationally?**
ShopDesk Plus is currently available to customers in the United States and Canada. International expansion of the membership program is planned but no release date has been confirmed. International customers can subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when ShopDesk Plus becomes available in their region.

**Can I pause my ShopDesk Plus membership?**
ShopDesk does not currently offer a membership pause option. If you wish to temporarily stop your membership, you will need to cancel and re-subscribe when you are ready. Re-subscribing after cancellation will start a new billing cycle at the current membership price, which may differ from your original rate.

**Does ShopDesk Plus include any product discounts?**
In addition to free shipping, ShopDesk Plus members receive an automatic 5% discount on all purchases. This discount is applied at checkout automatically when you are logged into your member account. The member discount can be combined with most coupon codes unless the coupon terms state otherwise.

**What is the ShopDesk Plus priority support benefit?**
ShopDesk Plus members have access to a dedicated support queue with guaranteed response times of under 2 hours during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM EST). Standard support response times are within 24 hours. Priority support covers all the same issues as standard support with faster resolution targets.

## Account Management

**How do I create a ShopDesk account?**
Visit shopdesk.com and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password of at least 8 characters. You will receive a verification email — click the link inside to activate your account. The verification link expires after 24 hours.

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter the email address associated with your account. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes for security purposes. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

**How do I update my email address or phone number?**
Log into your account and navigate to "Account Settings." From there you can update your contact information. Changes to your email address will require verification via a confirmation link sent to your new email address before the change takes effect.

**How do I add or update a delivery address?**
Log into your account and navigate to "Address Book" under Account Settings. You can add multiple delivery addresses and set a default address for future orders. Address changes do not affect orders that have already been placed.

**How do I delete my account?**
To request account deletion, contact our support team directly. Please note that account deletion is permanent and irreversible — all order history, saved addresses, and account data will be removed. Any outstanding orders must be completed or cancelled before an account can be deleted. Refunds for outstanding orders will still be processed to your original payment method even after deletion.

**Can I have multiple ShopDesk accounts?**
ShopDesk's terms of service permit one account per individual. Creating multiple accounts to exploit promotional offers or first-time buyer discounts is a violation of our terms and may result in all associated accounts being suspended.

**How do I opt out of marketing emails?**
You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any ShopDesk marketing email. You can also manage your email preferences under "Notifications" in your Account Settings. Note that unsubscribing from marketing emails does not affect transactional emails such as order confirmations and dispatch notifications.
